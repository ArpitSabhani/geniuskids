geniuskid

Genius Kid (GK) is an educational structured program of extracurricular study designed in the part classroom and part Ed-tech course format to provide individuals with the knowledge, skills, and competencies necessary to work in the field of mathematics, memory, chess, and cubing. These courses are typically offered to ages 3 to 16 years of age.

GK courses can be pursued at various academic levels and what makes it interesting is that it has the goodness of Ed-Tech with fun software for unlimited training while at the same time encouraging classroom learning so that the cons of being hooked to a digital screen is discouraged. This also enables our course mentors to give customized attention to every student as every brain is different. This enables a student to ask questions and develop inter and intra-personal relationships with social skills by meeting like-minded peers.

We believe in “building.” Building the community of mind sports, the interest in subjects usually assumed to be disliked by students, a team of educators interested in offering the knowledge, and a healthy chain of franchisees who join us in reaching out to a maximum number of students across the world.

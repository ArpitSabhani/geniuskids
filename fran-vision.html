FOUNDERS VISION

We are looking for franchisees who can keep the ball rolling and not just be another statistic. Any business venture in the world will require you to be organized, stay flexible, automate as many things as possible, limit your distractions, prioritize customer support, think of offering a seamless experience, be passionate about your work and lastly keep it simple and have fun. Business success looks different for every entrepreneur. Franchisee owners should set regular goals that evolve as the business grows. According to conventional wisdom, an annual ROI of approximately 7% or greater is considered good; we at Genius Kid aim to support you with having four times more.

Eusebius Noronha Founder and Chairperson - Genius Kid Genius Kid’s vision is the long-term strategic plan and overarching goals set by the company that owns and operates the franchise system. This vision serves as a guiding framework for the entire franchise network and provides direction for its growth, development, and overall success

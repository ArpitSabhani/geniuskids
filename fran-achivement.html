ACHIVEMENTS

Success means different to different people. Our founders believe that whatever you aim becomes your goal and achieving that is success. A person who wishes to be a stay-at-home parent and ensures their kids are happy, healthy, and performing well at school/extracurricular activities has achieved success. For a person who wishes to be an astronaut, and gets an invitation to be part of NASA, is success and for a person who wishes to become a billionaire and establishes a business that yields that kind of money is a success while for a person who wishes to be a bus driver and efficiently drives, reaches on time, and takes care of all the safety rules, is a success.

Success is not just fame or money; Success can be absolutely anything you wish or desire to achieve and end up actually achieving it.

At Genius Kid, we have covered all areas of success, giving every student and parent to choose their own journey with us. No matter what path they chose, they will enjoy the journey and success is inevitable

In 20-plus years of existence, Genius Kid has achieved a lot in different areas. Hence, we have chosen just a few highlights and categorized them under titles that define success to different people. You may select what category of success attracts you or means to you and re-live our achieving moments with us while you get to know us and our history better.
